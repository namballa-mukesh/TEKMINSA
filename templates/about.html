ABOUT Tekminsa

Tekminsa was created to support students. Many students especially freshers are confused with how to start their respective course. Many students are unable to find a official platform to discuss their doubts with professors/trainers. Even though colleges/universities appointed respective technical club team, but most of the students fail to interact with them. And to sort these problems, Tekminsa project was initialized.

Who are we?

Tekminsa was a project initiated by students of Indian Institute Of Information Kalyani(IIIT Kalyani), which is one of the prestigious engineering institutes of India.

Vision

Create interaction between students of different colleges, professors, and technical club team of their respective college.
