A Technical platform for students/learners and professionals.
Tekminsa creates an interactive platform with technical club members/professors of your community.

How do I get started with Tekminsa?

Well, its pretty simple. First make sure that your community(university/college) is joined or not. If joined, create your account and for account confirmation by your community officials(technical club team/professors).

Icon

Why only Tekminsa?

Tekminsa creates an official and private space for its users. It creates a much better environment to interact with professors and technical club team of your community.

Icon

How to join your community?

You need to request your technical committee to join Tekminsa. Once your community is verified by officials, you can create your account under respective community.

Icon

Searching for a project partner?

If you are finding for a partner or group to get started with your project, Tekminsa always welcomes you, just create an account and request for a partner by sharing your idea.

Icon

I don't have a community!

It's not an issue if you don't have a community, because Tekminsa itself creates a community for you. Tekminsa always supports its members.

ABOUT Tekminsa

Tekminsa was created to support students. Many students especially freshers are confused with how to start their respective course. Many students are unable to find a official platform to discuss their doubts with professors/trainers. Even though colleges/universities appointed respective technical club team, but most of the students fail to interact with them. And to sort these problems, Tekminsa project was initialized.

What Tekminsa does?

After logging into Tekminsa, you can personalize your profile for a better experience. It updates technical feed day-to-day, you can connect with people and see their ideas, posts shared. You can make discussions and chats with your trainers.
